A software assistant for ad managers

Planning, trafficking, and optimization executed by software, managed by humans.

A new type of AI for advertising

Hyper turbocharges ad managers. Instead of generating new audience targets, Hyper is focused on finding the most productive set of existing targets as quickly and efficiently as possible. It emulates the tactics of the best human ad managers but with superhuman speed, memory, and foresight.

Hyper provides leverage. A simple campaign specification consisting of budget, goals, and creatives is immediately expanded into a comprehensive multichannel media plan that includes a battery of A/B-style experiments across creative, audience target, and channel; all chosen using relevant historical performance as a guide. Over the lifetime of a campaign the platform will generate tens or hundreds of plan iterations, ranging from small budget adjustments to entirely new exploration efforts.

The technology

Hyper is built to operate on top of any buying system with an API, including “walled gardens” and traditional DSPs. The core platform is format agnostic and will make use of any native optimization capabilities.

It is based around two core technologies:

  • Hyper Ad Graph — a graph-based persistence layer; and
  • Hyper Learning Engine — a custom-built reinforcement learning AI.

All of this is made available via a lightweight web-based campaign management UI that includes interactive campaign planning, a newsfeed-based notification system, and cross-channel reporting.
