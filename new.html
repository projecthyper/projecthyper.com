Hyper provides AI-assisted planning, trafficking, and optimization for digital campaigns

The product

Hyper, provides AI-driven assistance to ad managers. It combines planning, trafficking, and optimization into a unified, iterative process executed by a software agent under the guidance of humans. Instead of generating new audience targets, Hyper focuses on finding the most productive set of existing targets as quickly and efficiently as possible. It emulates the strategies of the best human ad managers but does so with superhuman speed, memory, and foresight. It increases the number of campaigns managed per person and the number and quality of experiments performed within each campaign.

The technology

Hyper is built to operate on top of any buying system with an API, including “walled gardens” and traditional DSPs. The core platform is format agnostic and will make use of any native optimization capabilities.

It is based around two core technologies:

  • Hyper Ad Graph — a graph-based persistence layer; and
  • Hyper Learning Engine — a custom-built reinforcement learning AI.

All of this is made available via a lightweight web-based campaign management UI that includes interactive campaign planning, a newsfeed-based notification system, and cross-channel reporting.
